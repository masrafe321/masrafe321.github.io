FROM THE FOUNDER

Hospitality Heart, Digital Mind

Hi, I’m Glenn Philips, the founder of LensCraft Studio. My roots are planted in the bustling world of hospitality where I spent countless hours ensuring that every interaction feels like a warm welcome. Since stepping into the digital realm,my journey has been fulled by a fascination with the Internet's boundless possibilities that shape how we interact with one another online on a daily basis.

The vibrant chaos of hospitality taught me skills beyond managing a team in a fast paced, high pressure environment. The importance of genuine connections, clear communication and the art of making everyone feel at home were instilled from the get-go. These principles have been my compass while I navigated through the digital landscape, merging my love of social connection with my curiosity for the inner workings of the online world.

At Signal, we go beyond just creating cool websites; we’re crafting digital homes for your business that you'll be proud of, ensuring that everyone you want at the party feels welcomed.
